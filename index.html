Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Biomedical Engineering and Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at National University of Singapore. Before joining NUS, I was a senior research fellow at the Department of Computer Science, University College London, working with Prof. Danail Stoyanov. I received my Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu. I am listed at Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia, Class 2024.

My research interests include artificial intelligence and its applications on healthcare, with an emphasized application to medical image computing and robotic surgical data science. I recently work on multi-modality medical foundation model, video-language learning in surgery, resource efficient learning, and smart agent for robotic surgery with 3D reconstruction.

*Opening!* I am looking for PhD(2025Spring&Fall)/Postdoc/RA/Interns/Visiting students supported by CSC, who are interested in machine learning, medical image analysis, surgical 2D/3D vision, surgical robotic intelligence. Students with strong self-motivation and publication record are preferred. Welcome to email me after reading the details.

If you are an NUS student interested in doing research with me, also feel free to drop me an email!
